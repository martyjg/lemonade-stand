LEMONADE    STAND    INSTRUCTIONS

Objective: You are heavily indebted to the students' loan company. You have just 6 days to accumulate 5000. Despite having a university degree you have no discernible skills apart from the ability to produce some tasty lemonade. Opening a lemonade stand is your only hope of survival.

Each day you must purchase lemons, ice and sugar to make the lemonade. Ice lasts only 1 day. Lemons last 2 days. Sugar does not expire. You must also set the price of your lemonade daily. Too high and people will be less inclined to purchase. Too low and your profit will also be low. Sales are also effect by the weather. Warmer weather increases sales. You must also select a location to set up your lemonade stand. More popular locations increase sales but cost a greater fee to yourself.
